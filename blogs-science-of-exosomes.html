The Science of Exosomes in Modern Skincare

May 2025 · Préime Science Team · 5 min read

How exosome signalling is redefining regeneration — and what makes Préime's Pure Technology™ different.

Exosomes are nanoscale vesicles released by cells to communicate with one another — carrying growth factors, peptides and genetic signals that tell skin cells to repair, renew and regenerate. In the last decade they have moved from the research lab to the treatment room, becoming one of the most exciting frontiers in aesthetic skincare.

Why exosomes matter

Unlike topical actives that sit on the surface, exosomes work at the level of cellular signalling. They can accelerate healing after procedures, calm inflammation, and stimulate the skin's own regenerative machinery — supporting firmer, smoother, more radiant skin over time.

The Préime difference

Préime Pure delivers 33 million human-derived exosomes per vial, processed under our SCOPES standard to preserve potency. No cell-culture shortcuts, no animal-derived mediums — just a high concentration of intact, bioactive exosomes where the skin needs them most.

Paired with the Préime DermaFacial or NanoTap Pen for enhanced delivery, exosome therapy becomes a repeatable, measurable protocol rather than a one-off treatment.

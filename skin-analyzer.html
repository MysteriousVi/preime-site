What Is It?

The Préime Skin Analyzer is a smart imaging and AI-driven diagnostics platform designed to enhance skin assessments in aesthetic clinics, medspas, and dermatology practices.

By combining 8 spectral light imaging modes, 17 facial parameters, and predictive skin aging analytics, it offers a fast, reliable, and high-precision way to personalize skincare journeys — all in one click.

Core Technologies

17 Skin Parameters Tracked

  • Hydration
  • Sebum
  • Pigmentation (surface & deep)
  • Pores
  • Wrinkles
  • Collagen density
  • Acne
  • Blackheads
  • Porphyrins
  • Sensitivity (thermogram + rubein map)
  • Fluorescent agent residue
  • Mixed/brown/superficial pigment types

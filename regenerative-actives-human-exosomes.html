Human-Sourced Exosomes

Príme introduces Human-Sourced Exosomes, the latest nanobiotechnology innovation designed to support and regenerate the skin. These cutting-edge actives harness the power of umbilical cord plasma-derived exosomes, ensuring unparalleled purity, efficacy, and stability for optimal skin rejuvenation.

SCOPES System
Defining Pure Technology™

Préime Human Exosomes follow the SCOPES system, guaranteeing the highest standards of purity, performance, and regenerative efficacy.

Source

Umbilical cord plasma-derived exosomes derived from both epithelial and mesenchymal stem cells.

Concentration

Pure, allowing for greater efficiency and better results.

Origin

Ethically sourced, traceable, and clean cord blood.

Purity

Due to the patented extraction process, no extra processing which can damage the exosomes.

Efficacy

Clinically proven efficacy with less material, delivering visible skin regeneration.

Stability

Due to the patented pure extraction process, our exosomes require no freeze-drying, remain stable at room temperature, and have a 2-year shelf life.

Advanced Formulations for Optimal Results

Préime DermaCeuticals offers two advanced formulations of their Human-Sourced Exosomes:

  • Pure: 33 million exosomes per vial.
  • Pure Bright: 8 million exosomes per vial.

Through our exclusive extraction process, we eliminate the need for:

  • Cell culture or extra processing that can damage exosomes.
  • Foreign protein mediums such as Fetal Bovine Serum (FBS).

This ensures higher purity, increased bioavailability, and superior clinical performance in aesthetic and regenerative treatments.

About Préime Dermafacial

Préime Dermafacial

The Préime DermaFacial is a pioneer in combination therapy offering five powerful technologies, allowing the curation of personalised treatments. The Préime DermaFacial has been designed by skincare experts, for skincare experts. Focusing on efficacy, limitless treatment combinations and ease of use, the Préime DermaFacial is the primary skincare platform for clinics, aesthetic centres and medspas.

5 Powerful Technologies. 1 Intelligent Platform.

AquaB

Hydradermabrasion with Vacuum

Gently exfoliates, hydrates, and infuses skin with active ingredients using vortex-powered suction. Prepares skin for deeper treatments.

Benefit

Immediate smoothness, minimized pores, and enhanced glow.

VibroX

CO₂ Microdermabrasion + Vibration + Red LED

Resurfaces the skin with carbon-dioxide microdermabrasion while rhythmic vibration and calming red-LED light boost circulation and soothe the complexion — all in a single pass.

Benefit

Refined texture, a brighter tone, and visibly reduced redness.

MicroT

Bipolar Microcurrent (400Hz)

Delivers gentle bipolar microcurrent that re-educates and tones the facial muscles while re-energising skin cells for a natural, non-invasive lift.

Benefit

Firmer, lifted, and more sculpted facial contours.

Collagen+

Bipolar Radio Frequency (1MHz)

Warms the deeper layers of the skin with bipolar radio frequency, triggering the body's natural collagen and elastin renewal over the weeks that follow.

Benefit

Tighter, plumper, more youthful-looking skin.

UltraB

Ultrasound Infusion (1MHz)

Harnesses 1MHz ultrasound to drive targeted actives deep into the skin, maximising absorption and locking in the results of every treatment.

Benefit

Deeper hydration and noticeably enhanced product performance.

Combination Therapy
with a Smart Device

The Préime DermaFacial is a smart device, powered by IoT (Internet of Things) technology, designed to ensure consistent results and client satisfaction. This state-of-the-art system reveals a completely new approach in performing treatments, by delivering actual data to the aesthetic providers. This allows users to understand, examine and constantly improve their own methods and practice.

A Comprehensive 5-Step Treatment Process

Préime DermaFacial delivers a multi-step, results-driven skin rejuvenation process — maximizing benefits with minimal downtime.

Deeply cleanse and prep the skin while infusing the first layer of active ingredients, creating the ideal canvas for treatment.

Exfoliate and revive the skin with bespoke microdermabrasion technology that supports skin oxygenation. The skin will be cleansed and oxygenated, resulting in a re-energized appearance.

Bipolar microcurrent and radio frequency work together to tone facial muscles and tighten the skin for a lifted, sculpted finish.

Stimulate collagen and rejuvenate at the cellular level, restoring elasticity and a healthy, youthful glow.

Seal in results with deeply nourishing, ultrasound-infused actives that lock in hydration and protect the skin barrier.

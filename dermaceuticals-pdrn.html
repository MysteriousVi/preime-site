What Is It?

Préime PDRN Booster is a high-performance nucleotide solution designed to promote deep tissue support skin rejuvenation. regeneration and repair. Using Polydeoxyribonucleotide (PDRN) — a molecule 95% identical to human DNA — this formula boosts skin renewal, helps improve elasticity, and visibly refines skin tone and texture.

It’s an ideal choice for anti-aging protocols, post-laser recovery, and clients with compromised skin barriers.

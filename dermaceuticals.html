Préime DermaCeuticals are a range of professional-grade, regenerative skincare solutions — cocktails, boosters and advanced actives — formulated to be used as standalone treatments or to enhance other aesthetic procedures.

Unlike traditional skincare products, Préime DermaCeuticals are backed by clinical science and used in professional aesthetic settings. Each formula is precision-engineered to work in synergy with advanced aesthetic devices or as stand-alone protocols, offering targeted, regenerative outcomes.

From dullness, dehydration and uneven tone to fine lines, loss of firmness and post-procedure recovery, the range can be tailored to address a wide spectrum of concerns across every skin type.

Exosomes are tiny messenger vesicles that carry growth factors and proteins between cells. In skincare they signal the skin to repair and regenerate, accelerating healing and improving texture, tone and vitality.

PDRN (polydeoxyribonucleotide) is a regenerative active that stimulates tissue repair, boosts hydration and improves elasticity — making it ideal for rejuvenation and post-treatment recovery.

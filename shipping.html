Orders and Delivery

Shipping and Returns

General information for Préime provider orders, delivery queries, returns, and damaged items.

Last updated14 July 2026Questions?
01

Order processing

Provider orders are processed through authorised ordering channels. Availability, lead times, and any market-specific requirements are confirmed at the time of order.

02

Shipping

Delivery methods, estimated timelines, and costs vary by destination and are shown or confirmed before an order is completed.

03

Returns

Eligible unopened products may be returned within 30 days, subject to market-specific terms and prior authorisation from your account team.

04

Damaged or incorrect items

Report damaged, incomplete, or incorrect deliveries within seven days of receipt and include the order reference and supporting photographs where possible.

05

Devices and service items

Device returns, service parts, and warranty claims follow separate procedures. Contact technical support before sending any equipment.

06

Need help?

Contact your account manager or email info@emaltd.com before arranging a return so the team can provide the correct instructions.

The Power Of Plant-Derived Exosomes

Plant Exosomes

Príme introduces Plant-Based Exosomes—an advanced innovation in synthetic nanobiotechnology, designed to enhance skin regeneration. These advanced actives are derived from highly purified botanical sources, offering a sustainable and effective approach to skin rejuvenation and repair.

SCOPES System
Defining Pure Technology™

Préime Plant Exosomes follow the SCOPES system, guaranteeing the highest standards of purity, performance, and regenerative efficacy.

Source

Derived from "Gala" apple pulp extract, mandarin orange pulp extract, and papaya pulp extract.

Concentration

Highly purified extract containing 15 million plant exosomes for superior efficacy.

Origin

Organically grown and purified in Italy, ensuring sustainable, high-quality botanical sourcing.

Purity

Extracted through filtration and purification stages to isolate Extracellular Vesicles (EVs) while maintaining integrity.

Efficacy

Ongoing clinical studies are evaluating its powerful regenerative properties, further validating its efficacy.

Stability

Thanks to the patented extraction process, plant-based exosomes do not require freeze-drying and remain stable at room temperature with a 2-year shelf life.

Harnessing the Regenerative Power of Nature

Príme DermaCeuticals’ Plant-Based Exosomes offer a potent, sustainable alternative to traditional regenerative skincare. With a focus on high bioavailability and purity, these exosomes help

  • Enhance cellular renewal and skin repair
  • Support hydration and barrier function
  • Provide antioxidant protection against environmental stressors

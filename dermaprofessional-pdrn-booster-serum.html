Préime PDRN Booster Serum — Daily Rejuvenating Face Serum

A lightweight yet potent serum powered by PDRN to visibly improve skin texture, hydration, and overall radiance — ideal for post-treatment care and daily regeneration.

What Is It?

Préime PDRN Booster Serum is a high-performance daily serum formulated with polydeoxyribonucleotide (PDRN) — a DNA-based active clinically proven to support skin regeneration rejuvenation, repair, and hydration.

It’s an ideal choice for aging skin, dull complexion, texture irregularities, or clients recovering from in-clinic aesthetic procedures. With its light texture and strong clinical performance, this serum bridges the gap between high-level aesthetics and daily skincare.

Who it's for

Préime PDRN Booster Serum is ideal for:

  • ✓ Aging or tired skin
  • ✓ Clients recovering from in-clinic treatments
  • ✓ Post-laser, RF, or microneedling maintenance
  • ✓ Uneven texture or loss of elasticity
  • ✓ Skin in need of daily hydration and renewal

Key Ingredients

PDRN

Promotes cellular regeneration and tissue repair, reduces fine lines and improves tone.

Glutathione

Helps fade discoloration and brighten the complexion.

Hydrating Complex

Supports daily skin moisture levels and elasticity.

Lightweight Formula

Absorbs quickly without residue — ideal for layering under SPF or makeup.

How to Use

Préime DermaProfessional helps extend the value of in-clinic care by:

  1. •After toner (PDRN or Pure Elixir), apply 1–2 pumps to face and neck
  2. •Gently massage until fully absorbed
  3. •Follow with moisturizer
  4. •Use morning and night, especially post-procedure

Safe to use immediately after in-clinic treatments

Pairs well with Préime Moisturizer or Eye Cream

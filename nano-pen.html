Préime NanoTap Pen

What Is It?

The Préime NanoTap Pen is an advanced cosmetic delivery tool that gently taps the skin's surface using nanosized metal tips instead of needles. It creates temporary micro-channels in the epidermis, enhancing the absorption of active skincare ingredients without piercing the skin.

This makes it ideal for:

  • ✓ Sensitive areas (under eyes, lips, neck)
  • ✓ Clients avoiding downtime or needles
  • ✓ Fast-absorption protocols with exosomes, peptides, or PDRN
  • ✓ Designed to complement Préime DermaCeuticals for maximum efficacy.

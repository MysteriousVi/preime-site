The Origin of Préime

Préime [pronounced pre-may] is more than a name — it's a philosophy. Inspired by the Gaelic word préimhe, meaning premium, Préime reflects our unwavering commitment to delivering high-quality, high-value skincare solutions, powered by innovation and science.

Founded by EMA Aesthetics, Préime was born from the belief that beauty is deeply personal — a journey that should be bold, expressive, and confidently yours.

We listened to modern individuals share their stories, struggles, and aspirations — and transformed those insights into a science-led skincare system designed to empower every reflection.

From our multi-technology DermaFacial systems, to our performance-driven DermaCeuticals formulations, to the DermaProfessional line for professional-grade skincare at home — Préime offers a complete ecosystem that brings expert care into every setting: clinic, med-spa, or home.

Wherever you are in your journey, Préime is designed to help you reflect your best self — backed by science, inspired by you. Because at Préime, every skin tone, every story, and every reflection matters.
